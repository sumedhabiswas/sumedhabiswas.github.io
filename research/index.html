PhD Research
Radboud University · 2020–2026
1

EM Counterparts of GW Events

Observing strategies with Gaia (GaiaX) and MeerLICHT/BlackGEM to detect kilonovae and afterglows from LVK binary mergers during O4. Characterised 11,000+ GaiaX transient candidates ahead of O4.

2

Dark Sirens & the Hubble Constant

Performance verification for determining H₀ and identifying host galaxies of GW events using WEAVE spectroscopic data. Bayesian statistical framework for dark standard sirens with LVK O4 BBH events.

3

Fast X-ray Transients & Magnetars

Comparing the volumetric space densities of millisecond-spin magnetars and fast X-ray transients to probe evolutionary channels and common-envelope physics.

4

Fast X-ray Transients & Dark GRBs

Exploring the connection between fast X-ray transients and dark gamma-ray bursts, probing the progenitor population and the role of dust extinction in obscured transients.

5

Host Galaxies with 3G Detectors

Determining the host galaxies of binary black hole mergers using the improved sky localisation from third-generation GW detector networks (Einstein Telescope, Cosmic Explorer).

Masters Research
Nikhef, Amsterdam · 2019–2020
1

Primordial Black Holes in the Lower Mass Gap

Distinguishing primordial and astrophysical binary black holes in the lower mass gap using GW parameter estimation, probing dark matter as sub-solar mass compact objects.

2

Sub-Solar Mass GW Searches

Improving matched-filter search pipelines for sub-solar mass primordial black holes in LIGO/Virgo data, including template bank design and sensitivity characterisation.

S. Biswas, Z. Kostrzewa-Rutkowska, P. G. Jonker, P. Vreeswijk, D. Eappachen, P. J. Groot, S. Hodgkin et al.
